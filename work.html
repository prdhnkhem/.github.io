The Work: The HUNATE Movement

This is where philosophy meets the soil. The HUNATE Movement is the work of my life—a citizen-led initiative to build a future that is regenerative by design.

My First Mission: The 7-7-7 Initiative

A vision is only as powerful as the action it inspires. My first mission is a direct, on-the-ground plan to plant 14 seeds of change across Nepal, creating living proof that a better world is possible.

The plan is simple and profound:

  • I will partner with 7 brave families and 7 forward-thinking orphanages.
  • We will establish these 14 "Hunateculture Hubs" across the 7 provinces of Nepal.

Over five years, my team and I will guide each hub through a complete life redesign using the principles of Hunateculture. They will become lighthouses in their communities—fully self-reliant in food, health, and energy, and serving as real-world classrooms for others to learn from. This is not charity; this is a shared investment in human potential and ecological regeneration.

The Architects of Change: The Team I Am Building

This work is too big for one person. It requires a dedicated family of visionaries, builders, and guardians. I am gathering a team that embodies the multi-skilled, self-reliant spirit of the Hunatian.

Hunate Engineers

A core team of young, multi-skilled "life designers"—architects, permaculturists, astrologers, and healers—who will work directly with our partner hubs to implement the Hunateculture blueprint.

Hunate Mentors

The guardians of wisdom. Respected elders and professionals who will provide ethical guidance and deep experience to my Engineers, ensuring our work is grounded in integrity and truth.

The Foundation: The "Becoming Hunatian" Course

Before one can join my core team, one must do the inner work. I have designed "Becoming Hunatian," a free, 3-month public course for anyone who wants to begin this journey of self-transformation.

It is a path to unlearn the conditioning of the failing system and to reclaim your own health, purpose, and inner wisdom. You can start this journey today. The course is my open-source gift to anyone with the courage to begin.
