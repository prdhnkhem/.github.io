Join the Work

This is not a job offer. It is an invitation to a shared mission. This is a call to those who are ready to dedicate their skills, heart, and energy to building a new world.

I. A Call for Hunate Engineers

I am seeking a core team of young (under 30), multi-skilled, and self-reliant individuals to become the first Hunate Engineers. You will be the life designers and the architects of this new world, guiding our partner hubs through their transformation. This is the most demanding and most rewarding role in the movement.

This is a path of service, not salary. We operate on a model of shared investment and radical trust. If you are looking for a job, this is not the right place. If you are looking for your life's work, you may have found it.

Apply to Become an Engineer

II. A Search for Hunate Hubs

I am searching for 7 families and 7 orphanages in Nepal to become our first pioneers. You will be the living proof that a self-sufficient, regenerative life is possible. This is a profound, five-year partnership where I and my team will provide the knowledge and guidance, and you will provide the commitment, the land, and the heart to walk this path with us.

Apply to Become a Hub

III. An Invitation for Mentors

A movement without the wisdom of its elders is a tree without roots. I am actively seeking the guidance of respected professionals and community leaders to serve as Mentors. Your experience is the compass that will keep our work grounded in integrity, excellence, and long-term vision. This is a call to the guardians of wisdom.

Offer Your Guidance

IV. A Space for Volunteers

Once our hubs are active, we will need skilled hands and open hearts on the ground. This is an invitation for those who want to learn by doing and contribute to a real-world project. This role requires a minimum commitment of three months and a willingness to immerse yourself in the Hunateculture lifestyle.

Register to Volunteer

V. A Need for District Representatives

To ensure our work is deeply connected to the unique reality of each community, I am looking for representatives in every district of Nepal. You will be our eyes, our ears, and our trusted bridge to local wisdom, needs, and opportunities. This is a vital role for ensuring our blueprint is built with the people, not just for them.

Represent Your District
