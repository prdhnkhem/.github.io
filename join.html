Work With Me

This is not a job offer. It is an invitation to a shared mission. I am gathering a family of co-creators who are ready to dedicate their skills, heart, and energy to building a new world.

The Call for Hunate Engineers

I am looking for a core team of young (under 30), multi-skilled, and self-reliant individuals to become the first Hunate Engineers. You will be the life designers and the architects of this new world. Your role is a vow to live the change you wish to see. You will be my closest collaborators, working with me to guide our partner hubs through their transformation.

This is a path of service, not salary. We operate on a model of shared investment and radical trust. If you are looking for a job, this is not the right place. If you are looking for your life's work, you may have found it.

I am looking for individuals with a deep passion and growing skill in areas like Permaculture, Eco-Architecture, Vastu, Ayurveda, Yoga, Vedic Astrology, and more. Above all, I am looking for a fierce commitment to truth and a humble heart.

Apply to be an Engineer

The Search for Hunate Hubs

I am searching for 7 families and 7 orphanages in Nepal to become our first pioneers. You will be the living proof that a self-sufficient, regenerative life is possible. This is a profound partnership. I will bring my team and the full toolkit of Hunateculture to help you redesign your life, your land, and your livelihood. In return, I ask for your trust, your commitment, and your courage to walk this path with us.

I am looking for partners who:

  • Have legal, debt-free access to at least 1 Bigha of land.
  • Possess a deep, unshakable desire to become self-reliant and live in harmony with nature.
  • Are willing to become a learning center—a lighthouse—for their local community.
