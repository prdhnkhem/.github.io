Join the Movement

This is not a job offer. It is an invitation to a shared mission. I am gathering a family of co-creators who are ready to dedicate their skills, heart, and energy to building a new world.

The Call for Hunate Engineers

I am seeking a core team of young (under 30), multi-skilled, and self-reliant individuals to become the first Hunate Engineers. You will be the life designers and the architects of this new world, guiding our partner hubs through their transformation. This is the most demanding and most rewarding role in the movement.

I am not looking for employees with a specific degree; I am looking for co-creators who have a deep, lived understanding of their craft and an unwavering commitment to a better future. You must be willing to unlearn as much as you learn, and to put service before self.

This is a path of service, not salary. We operate on a model of shared investment and radical trust. If you are looking for a job, this is not the right place. If you are looking for your life's work, you may have found it.

Apply to Become an Engineer

The Search for Hunate Hubs

I am searching for 7 families and 7 orphanages in Nepal to become our first pioneers. You will be the living proof that a self-sufficient, regenerative life is possible. This is a profound, five-year partnership. I will bring my team and the full toolkit of Hunateculture to help you redesign your life, your land, and your livelihood. In return, I ask for your trust, your commitment, and your courage to walk this path with us.

I am looking for partners who:

  • Have legal, debt-free access to at least 1 Bigha of land.
  • Possess a deep, unshakable desire to become self-reliant and live in harmony with nature.
  • Are willing to become a learning center—a lighthouse—for their local community.
