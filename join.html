Find Your Place in the Movement

This is a call to the visionaries, the builders, the pioneers, and the guardians. The future is not a spectator sport. It is time to find your role and build it with us.

For Hunate Engineers: The Life Designers

We are seeking a core team of 13 young (under 30), multi-skilled, and deeply committed individuals to become the first Hunate Engineers. You are the architects of this new world. Your role is not just a job; it is a vow to live the change you wish to see. You will be trained, you will be challenged, and you will guide our partner hubs in transforming their lives.

Who We Are Looking For:

  • Individuals with skills in: Permaculture, Eco-Architecture, Vastu, Ayurveda, Yoga, Astrology, Law, Finance, Media, IT, and more.
  • Those who are self-reliant, master of their own lives, and ready to work without a salary, driven by purpose and a shared investment in the future.
  • Brave souls ready to complete the "Becoming Hunatian" journey and dedicate themselves to a 5-year mission.
Apply to be an Engineer

For Hunate Hubs: The Pioneers

We are searching for 7 families and 7 orphanages across Nepal to become our first Hunateculture Hubs. You will be the living proof that a self-sufficient, regenerative life is possible. This is a deep partnership where we provide the knowledge, design, and guidance, and you provide the commitment, the land, and the heart.

Who We Are Looking For:

  • Farming families or orphanages with legal, debt-free access to at least 1 Bigha of land.
  • Those with a genuine desire to become self-reliant and live in harmony with nature.
  • Partners who are willing to invest in their own transformation and become a learning center for their community.
