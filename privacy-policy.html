Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 22, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how khemrajpradhan.com.np (the "Site," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your Personal Information when you visit the Site or interact with our services.

Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information you provide directly: When you fill out one of our Google Forms (e.g., for applications or recording donations), we collect the information you provide, such as your name, email address, and any other details you share.
  • Information collected automatically: Like most websites, we may use services like Cloudflare and potentially basic analytics to collect non-personally identifying information, such as your browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. The purpose of this is to better understand how visitors use our website and to improve performance and security.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Communicate with you regarding your applications, donations, or inquiries.
  • Maintain transparent records of support for the Hunate Movement.
  • Understand and analyze how our Site is used, in order to improve its content and functionality.
  • Maintain the security and integrity of our Site.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to operate our website. These services have their own privacy policies.

  • Google Forms: When you submit information through our forms, that information is stored on Google's servers. You can view Google's Privacy Policy here.
  • GitHub Pages: Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages. You can view their privacy information here.
  • Cloudflare: We use Cloudflare for security and performance. You can view Cloudflare's Privacy Policy here.

Data Security

We are committed to protecting your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you and to ask that your personal information be corrected, updated, or deleted. If you would like to exercise this right, please contact us through the information provided on our contact page.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time in order to reflect, for example, changes to our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage visitors to frequently check this page for any changes.

Contact Us

For more information about our privacy practices, if you have questions, or if you would like to make a complaint, please contact us by email at email@khemrajpradhan.com.np.
