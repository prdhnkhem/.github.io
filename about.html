/ GENESIS :

Born into a Newari-Brahmin rebel family in the hills of Nepal. Raised care free without decipline by grandparents, forging a warrior spirit in the shadows of civil war.

/ ISOLATION :

Seperation from parents at 2 and 12 years of hostel isolation. Education in Christian schools while living in Muslim communities. Early exposure to the extremes of human lust, desire, and systemic abuse.

/ SHADOWS :

Experienced incarceration, financial loss, family breakups and the depths of chemical addiction. Through these shadows, I initiated a process of self-healing, evolution, and radical deconstruction of the self.

/ EXPLORATION :

Hitchhiked without money, traveled solo across borders, and lived and worked with multiple partners from around the world like India, Cambodia, USA, China, France, Germany, etc. Learned many handy skills.

/ DISCOVERY :

Self-learned basics and concepts of the ancient scripts: Jyotish, Vastu, Ayurveda, and Yoga Sutras. Combined these with modern Scince, Permaculture design, architect, computers and technological tool mastery.

/ DETACHMENT :

Learned a way to utilize the profit-based economy system and not be consumed by it. Chose to become a bias-free observer, sacrificing social norms to protect the integrity of human and environmental systems.
