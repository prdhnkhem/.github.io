The Work Begins with You

The Hunate Movement is a community of co-creators. Each role is vital to the whole. This is more than a position; it is a path. Find yours below and answer the call.

The Call for Life Designers: Become a Hunate Engineer

This is a call to the young visionaries, the multi-skilled builders, and the courageous hearts ready to dedicate their lives to a new way of being. A Hunate Engineer is not a job title; it is a sacred responsibility to co-create the blueprint for a regenerative future.

Who We Seek: We are looking for individuals under 30 with a deep passion, a humble spirit, and expertise in one of our 13 core domains. You must be a self-starter, a deep thinker, and ready to commit to a journey of intense learning and service.

The Path: The selection process is a journey of mutual discovery, involving a detailed application, a first assignment to test your vision, and a final alignment dialogue. This is not for everyone; it is for the truly called.

Apply to be an Engineer

The Call for Wisdom Keepers: Become a Hunate Mentor

A new generation's energy must be guided by the embodied wisdom of its elders. We are seeking a council of respected leaders and experts to serve as the ethical compass and strategic guardians of our movement. This is not a role of management, but of sacred guidance.

Who We Seek: Esteemed professionals and practitioners in our core domains who feel a deep responsibility to pass on their life's learnings. Your role is high-impact, requiring only a few hours a month.

The Path: Our engagement begins with a respectful dialogue to ensure philosophical alignment, followed by an in-person meeting to formalize the partnership. This is an invitation to shape a legacy.

Express Interest as a Mentor

The Call for Lighthouses: Become a Hunate Hub

We are seeking 14 courageous partners—7 families and 7 orphanages—to embark on a profound, multi-year journey of transformation. Your home and land will become a living example of a self-sufficient, regenerative way of life for your entire community.

Who We Seek: Partners with secure land access, a deep desire for self-reliance, a willingness to work hard, and an open heart to learn and share. You will be the living proof that a new world is possible.

The Path: A careful selection process involving a detailed application, a site visit, an in-depth interview, and a final decision by our Weighted Council ensures a deep and lasting partnership.

Apply for Partnership

The Call for Co-Creators: Become a Volunteer

This is an invitation to get your hands in the soil. We are looking for passionate individuals to join us on the ground for a minimum of 3 months. This is not voluntourism; it is a deep dive into community living, hard work, and the practical application of Hunateculture.

Who We Seek: Resilient, adaptable individuals with practical skills (in farming, building, teaching, or media) and a spirit of service. You must be ready for simple living and a profound, life-changing experience.

The Path: An application followed by an alignment interview to ensure your expectations and skills are a good fit for the needs of our hubs.

Apply to Volunteer

The Call for Living Bridges: Become a District Representative

The Hunate Movement is built on deep local wisdom. We are seeking trusted individuals to be our eyes, ears, and heart in every district of Nepal. This is a sacred role of service, connection, and trust.

Who We Seek: Individuals with deep, respected roots in their community, excellent communication skills, and a profound understanding of the local culture. Your integrity is your most important qualification.

The Path: A process involving an application, a first assignment to assess your local insight, and an alignment dialogue.

Apply to be a DR
