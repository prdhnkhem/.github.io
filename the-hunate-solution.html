The Hunate Solution

To heal a failing system, we cannot just fix the broken pieces. We must create a new, living model. This is our blueprint for a regenerative future, from the individual to the entire planet.

The Four Layers of Our Solution

Our solution is a comprehensive, multi-layered approach that addresses the crisis at every level—from the inner world of the individual to the collective action of a global movement.

The Philosophy: Our North Star

The core of our solution is a simple, profound idea: restoring the sacred balance between HUmanity, NAture, and TEchnology. This is our guiding philosophy, a research and development project to find regenerative ways of life.

The Individual: Becoming Hunatian

True change starts within. The "Becoming Hunatian" course is our answer for the individual—a free, self-guided journey to reclaim inner sovereignty and unlearn harmful conditioning. It is the first step.

Begin the Course

The Community: Hunateculture

For the family and community, we offer Hunateculture. This is our practical design science that blends ancient wisdom (Vastu, Ayurveda) with modern ethics (Permaculture, Eco-Architecture) to redesign homes, farms, and businesses for self-sufficiency and harmony.

The World: The Hunate Movement

For the global family, we offer the Hunate Movement. It is our citizen-led campaign to spread awareness and share the Hunateculture blueprint. It is how we unite to take collective action for our shared future.

Our First Mission: The 7-7-7 Initiative

To bring this vision to life, we begin at home. The 7-7-7 Initiative is our tangible, on-the-ground plan to co-create **14 "Hunateculture Hubs"**—partnering with **7 Families and 7 Orphanages** across all **7 Provinces of Nepal**. These hubs will be living proof that this new way of life is possible, serving as lighthouses of resilience for the entire nation.

Our Future Vision: The Hunate HQ

After the 7-7-7 Initiative is established, our next great work will be to build the Hunate HQ. This will be our permanent home, our school, and a world-class example of a fully regenerative community—the physical embodiment of our vision for the future.
