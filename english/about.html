The Practical Foundation

The Joon Project

Before the philosophy was formalized, the work began on the ground. The Joon Project was a hands-on initiative working with orphanages and vulnerable communities. It was here that the limitations of charity and the deep systemic flaws in our social structures became painfully clear. This practical experience provided the invaluable, real-world data that proved a more holistic, self-sufficient model was not just a theory, but a necessity.

The Philosophical Culmination

The Hunate Project

Hunate is the synthesis of a life's lessons. It is the philosophy that emerged when personal experience met practical reality. It answers the question: "How do we move forward?" The project outlines the worldview, the core principles, and the vision for a future where humanity and nature are no longer in opposition. It is the intellectual and spiritual blueprint that guides the entire movement.
