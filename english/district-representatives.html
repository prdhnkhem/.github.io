Support Our District Representatives

Change happens at the community level. Our District Representatives are the local ambassadors who carry the Hunate vision into villages and towns across Nepal. Your support empowers their crucial grassroots work.

Why Support Our Representatives?

District Representatives are the essential link between the national movement and local communities. They are the organizers, communicators, and network-weavers who identify potential Hub Partners, host informational sessions, and build the relationships of trust that are necessary for true community-led change. Supporting them is an investment in the social fabric of the movement.

Where Your Contribution Goes

Funds designated for District Representatives are used to provide the practical resources they need to be effective in their communities:

  • Travel & Transportation: Costs for fuel and public transport to allow Reps to travel within their districts to meet with potential partners and communities.
  • Communication: Support for phone and internet costs to ensure they can stay connected with the central team and their local network.
  • Outreach Materials: Funds for printing flyers, informational booklets, and other materials to share the Hunate vision.
  • Community Events: Support for organizing small, local gatherings and workshops to introduce Hunate principles to the public.
  • Gifts: Small tokens of appreciation for the immense value and expertise our DRs contribute.
  • Fun & Celebration: Resources to foster a healthy, joyful, and collaborative team culture.
