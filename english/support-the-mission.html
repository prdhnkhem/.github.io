Support the Mission: Fuel the Future

The Hunate Movement is a grassroots, community-funded initiative. We do not accept funding that compromises our vision. This means every contribution, no matter the size, is a vital act of trust and co-creation that allows us to do this important work.

Your Support Builds a Regenerative World

Empowering Our Hubs

Your funds directly support our partner families and orphanages by providing essential construction tools, heirloom seeds for their gardens, and the initial resources to launch their ethical, regenerative family businesses.

Equipping Our Team

Contributions cover the modest travel and research expenses for our core team of Engineers and District Reps, allowing them to conduct site visits, gather data, and provide hands-on guidance to our Hub Partners.

Sustaining Our Volunteers

Your support helps us provide simple, healthy food and basic accommodation for the international and local volunteers who bring their invaluable skills and energy to help build our hubs from the ground up.
