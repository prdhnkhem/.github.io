The Hub Partners: Beacons of the New World

The Hub Partners are the very heart of the Hunate Movement. They are the brave families and orphanages who provide the land, the commitment, and the spirit to build the first living, breathing examples of Hunateculture for the world to see.

More Than a Farm: A Center for Community Regeneration

Becoming a Hub Partner is a commitment to a profound transformation. You are not just creating a self-sufficient farm; you are becoming a community leader, an educator, and a guardian of a new way of living. Your home will transform into a lighthouse for your entire region—a place of learning, hope, and tangible proof that a better, more beautiful world is possible. You will host volunteers from around the world, run workshops for your neighbors, and create an ethical family business that regenerates your land and your local economy. This is a path for those who are ready to move beyond survival and become a source of inspiration for all.

The Foundations: Core Criteria for Our Co-Creators

To ensure the success and integrity of our first hubs, we have established clear, non-negotiable criteria for our potential partners:

  • Must have legal, loan-free access to a minimum of 2 Bigha (approximately 1.35 hectares or 3.3 acres) of land.
  • Must be a family unit of around 5 members (joint families are a priority) or a registered orphanage.
  • Must have some practical, hands-on farming experience.
  • The land must be within a reasonable distance of a major city (approx. 10km radius or a 45-minute drive).
  • Must be willing to be a public "Hunateculture Hub Partner" and serve as a center for learning in your community.
  • Must have a clear and deep understanding that this is a partnership of knowledge, design, and support—not a financial grant or charity project.

Our Sacred Covenant: A Partnership of Mutual Commitment

This is a journey we walk side-by-side as equals. Our partnership is built on a foundation of mutual vows and shared responsibility.

Our Vow to You

The Hunate Movement commits to providing our Hub Partners with the most valuable resources we possess: a complete, personalized Hunateculture Life Design Blueprint; expert training and hands-on guidance from our dedicated team of Engineers and Mentors; and access to our global network of skilled volunteers and supporters.

Your Vow to the Mission

The Hub Partner, in return, provides the most essential elements that money cannot buy: the land, the heart, an unwavering commitment to learn and de-condition from old habits, and the courage to stand as a living example of a new way for your community and for the world.

A 5-Year Transformational Journey

Deep change takes time. Our partnership is a formal commitment to a five-year journey of learning, building, and growing together. We also understand that life is unpredictable. A "Graceful Exit Protocol" is built into our covenant, ensuring that if circumstances change, our disengagement is handled with the same trust and mutual respect on which the partnership was founded.
