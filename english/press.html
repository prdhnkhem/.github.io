September 22, 2025 | DAMAK, NEPAL

Hunate Movement Announces Flagship "7-7-7 Initiative" to Build Regenerative Hubs Across Nepal

The Hunate Movement today officially announced its flagship mission, the "7-7-7 Initiative," a groundbreaking plan to establish seven self-sustaining community hubs across the seven provinces of Nepal. Each hub will be designed using the principles of Hunateculture to support seven local families and seven orphanages, creating a powerful, scalable model for national resilience and regeneration.

Khem Raj Pradhan, founder of the movement, described the initiative as "the first tangible step in building a new system from the ground up." He added, "We are not waiting for change; we are creating it. Each hub will be a living example of a future where communities are self-reliant, ecologically balanced, and deeply interconnected." The movement is now actively seeking Hub Partners, Engineers, and Mentors to bring this vision to life.

September 15, 2025 | ONLINE

"Becoming Hunatian" Course Opens for Global Enrollment, Offering a Blueprint for Personal Transformation

The Hunate Movement has released its foundational educational course, "Becoming Hunatian," to a global audience via its YouTube channel. The course offers a free, chapter-by-chapter guide for individuals seeking to cultivate self-awareness, question societal conditioning, and redesign their lives based on principles of purpose and resilience.

The curriculum is a series of practical tasks focused on self-observation, understanding one's past, and redesigning present habits. "Global change is the cumulative effect of individual awakenings," said founder Khem Raj Pradhan. "This course provides the tools for that first, most crucial step."
