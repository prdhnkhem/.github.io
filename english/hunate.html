Hunate Philosophy

Hunate is not a belief system, an organization, or a utopian dream. It is a research and development project, a practical, open-source design science dedicated to restoring balance and harmony among humanity, nature, and technology.

THE 'WHY': A Diagnosis of Our Failing World

We exist because the systems we have inherited are failing. They are failing our children, our communities, our planet, and our own souls. We are born into a world that profits from our disconnection, and we spend our lives running on a treadmill of manufactured desire, wondering why we feel so empty. Hunate begins by asking the questions that this system forbids.

The Education System: Why do we send our children to schools for 15 years, only for them to emerge as anxious, debt-ridden specialists, disconnected from nature and their own innate wisdom? We have created a system that teaches compliance over creativity, memorization over critical thinking, preparing cogs for a machine that is already obsolete.

"We were born into a world that profits from our disconnection."

The Food & Health System: Why is the food that is cheapest and most accessible also the most toxic? We have built an agricultural system that poisons our soil and a healthcare system that profits from managing chronic disease rather than cultivating vibrant health. We are sold cures for the illnesses caused by the very products our economy produces.

The Social & Economic System: Why do we work our entire lives to build homes we barely live in, for families we barely see? Our economic model demands infinite growth on a finite planet, engineering loneliness and consumption as its primary fuel. It tells us that community is a luxury and that the natural world is a resource to be extracted, not a relative to be respected.

The Project Charter

Our Vision: A world where humanity, nature, and technology co-exist in a state of regenerative balance and creative harmony.

Our Mission: To research, develop, and openly share a practical, adaptable blueprint for designing regenerative lifestyles, homes, communities, and social systems based on the universal laws of nature.

Our Core Goals:

  • To establish living, breathing models of Hunateculture that others can learn from.
  • To provide a transformative educational path for individuals to heal, grow, and become conscious creators of their world.
  • To spark a global, grassroots movement of families and communities dedicated to building a regenerative future.
  • To remain an independent, non-political, and open-source research initiative.

THE 'HOW': System for Transformation

Hunate is not a single solution, but a multi-layered system where each part supports the whole. It is a logical progression from inner transformation to global change.

1. The Core Philosophy (Hunate): This is the foundation—the research and development engine. Hunate, as a philosophy, is the practice of observing the laws of nature and the flaws in our man-made systems to derive core principles for balanced living. It is the "why" and "how" behind everything else.

2. The Design System (Hunateculture): This is the practical application of the philosophy. Hunateculture is the tangible design system used to create regenerative homes, farms, families, and social structures. It is the direct, hands-on answer to the failing systems—the blueprint for the "what."

3. The Individual Path (Becoming Hunatian): To build a new world, we must first become new people. The "Becoming Hunatian" course is the essential journey of self-discovery, healing, and deconditioning that prepares an individual to live in a regenerative way. It builds the capable, conscious human who can successfully implement Hunateculture.

4. The Social Action (The Hunate Movement): Once the individual is transformed and the design is clear, the Hunate Movement is the vehicle to share these solutions with the world. It is the grassroots, community-based action to heal families, inspire neighbors, and spread the blueprint far and wide.

5. The First Step (Building 7 Provinces): The movement begins with a tangible, achievable mission. Building the first hubs in each of Nepal's 7 provinces is the real-world proof-of-concept, demonstrating that this entire system is not a dream, but an actionable plan for today.

The System at a Glance

Hunate (The Research) explores and discovers the principles.

Hunateculture (The Design) applies the principles.

Becoming Hunatian (The Person) embodies the principles.

The Movement (The Action) shares the principles.

THE PLAN: Our Phase Journey

PAST (The Foundation)

Phase 1: Personal Journey of Self Discovery

The decades-long personal quest that forged the core philosophical foundation of Hunate through direct experience.

Phase 2: The Joon Project

The real-world social experiment that tested, refined, and proved the Hunate principles in a community setting.

PRESENT (The Blueprint)

Phase 3: The Pause (2023-2026)

A strategic period of deep reflection, planning, and documentation to codify the Hunate blueprint and prepare for global outreach.

Phase 4: The Hunate Movement (Starting 2026)

The active formation of the core team, the search for the first hub partners, and the launch of the public movement.

FUTURE (The Expansion)

Phase 5: Building 7 Provinces

Our first tangible mission is to establish the initial 14 (7 families and 7 orphanages) Hunateculture hubs across Nepal.

Phase 6: Building Hunate HQ

The creation of the central, living model of a fully regenerative village, a global center for learning and innovation.

Phase 7: Building 77 Districts

The expansion of the model to every district in Nepal, creating a national network of regenerative communities.

Phase 8: Building 7 Continents

Sharing the Hunate blueprint and supporting its adaptation in communities across the globe.
