Phase 6

Building The Hunate HQ

Building The Hunate HQ

This is the ultimate goal of the Hunate Movement: the creation of a central, living model of a regenerative village that will serve as a global hub for learning, research, and innovation—a gift to the future.

The Hunate Center

More Than a Place, a Living Example

The Hunate HQ is not a corporate headquarters; it is the physical manifestation of our entire philosophy. After establishing our decentralized network of hubs across Nepal's seven provinces, we will bring all of that knowledge and experience together to build one central, world-class example of a fully regenerative community. It will be a place where families, orphans, mentors, and engineers converge to live, learn, build, and grow together. It is the living, breathing proof-of-concept for the world, demonstrating that a life of harmony, health, and purpose is not only possible but practical on a larger scale.

The Design: A Harmonious, Zone-Based Village

The layout of the Hunate Center will be a masterpiece of regenerative design, a village integrated seamlessly with nature. Using the principles of Vastu Shastra and Permaculture, the entire site will be organized into distinct but interconnected zones, each with its own purpose and energy, creating a harmonious and efficient flow of life.

  • The Community Core: The spiritual and social heart of the village, featuring the meditation hall, library, and central gathering spaces.
  • Living & Hospitality: Beautiful, natural-built huts for families, guests, and volunteers, designed for health and tranquility.
  • Workshops & Education: Dedicated spaces for learning practical skills in crafts, science, music, and art.
  • Food & Agriculture: A network of intelligent gardens, food forests, greenhouses, and ponds that provide abundant, nutrient-dense food for the entire community.
  • Energy & Utilities: The village's metabolic system, including biogas digesters, solar arrays, water filtration systems, and animal shelters.

The Zoning Philosophy

Each zone is assigned a color, reflecting its core energy and function, creating a visual and energetic map of the community.

  • Golden Yellow: Community Core
  • Earth Brown: Living & Hospitality
  • Terracotta Red: Workshops & Education
  • Leaf Green: Food & Agriculture
  • Cool Grey-Blue: Energy & Utilities

More Than a Village

A Global University for the Future

The Hunate HQ's ultimate purpose extends far beyond its physical borders. It will be a global university for the future of humanity. It is where people from all continents can come to learn Hunateculture not from a book, but by living it. It is the research lab where we will test and innovate new regenerative technologies and social systems. It is the mothership, the central heart that will pump knowledge, resources, and inspiration to the growing global network of Hunate Hubs. It is our living gift to the generations to come.
