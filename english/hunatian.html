The Course: Becoming Hunatian

Change must come from the self. This course is a structured journey designed to help you understand your mind, body, and soul, and to rewrite the thought patterns and habits that limit your potential.

The First Step: Know Thyself

Before you can change the world, you must understand your own. How do you function? What are you made of? How have you been shaped by your environment, parents, school, and society? The journey of "Becoming Hunatian" is the process of peeling back these layers of conditioning to find your authentic self.

This course is not about memorizing information; it is about conducting the most important research of your life: the study of you. By engaging in the daily tasks listed below, you will develop the skills of self-observation and self-reliance. **For full, detailed instructions on each task, please watch the corresponding video in our official YouTube playlist.**

"This is the foundational step. Global change is the cumulative effect of individual awakenings."

The Coursebook Structure

Watch the Full Course on YouTube

Chapter 1: The Foundation - Observing Self

This chapter builds the fundamental skill of non-judgmental awareness. The goal is to see yourself and your patterns clearly, without the filter of past conditioning.

  • Task 1: Claiming Your Sacred Ground
  • Task 2: Learn To Sit Still
  • Task 3: Observing Your Breath
  • Task 4: Observing Your Physical Body
  • Task 5: Observing Your Movements
  • Task 6: Observing Your Sense Organs
  • Task 7: Observing Your Surroundings
  • Task 8: Recall Before Sleep

Chapter 2: Understanding Environment

Here, you will explore the origins of your conditioning. By understanding how your environment shapes you, you can begin to choose your future consciously.

  • Task 9: Filter Kitchen Utensils
  • Task 10: Filter Cloth Materials
  • Task 11: Filter Materials Around You
  • Task 12: Manage Your Waste Production
  • Task 13: Filter Parents, Relatives and Friends
  • Task 14: Filter Teacher & School
  • Task 14: Filter Your Needs, Wants & Desires

Chapter 3: Redesigning Your Future

This chapter is about taking conscious control of your daily life. You will learn to design your habits, environment, and interactions to support your growth.

  • Task 15: Harvest As Many Skills As Possible As Human

A Lifelong Journey

"Becoming Hunatian" is not a course you finish, but a way of life you begin. The goal is to equip you with the tools to become a lifelong student of yourself and the world. For questions or support on your journey, you can reach out to us.
