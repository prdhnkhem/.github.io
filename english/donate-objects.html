Donate Objects: Contribute the Tools for Growth

Support for the Hunate Movement can come in many forms. Donating specific, needed items is a powerful and direct way to provide the tangible resources our teams need to build, learn, and grow.

How to Donate

If you have items from the lists below that you would like to contribute, please contact us first to coordinate. This helps us manage logistics and ensure we are not duplicating items. We are grateful for your generosity and commitment to supporting our work in this practical way.

Coordinate Your Donation

Equipment & Technology

These tools are essential for documenting our journey, managing our projects, and enabling our teams to work effectively.

Filming & Media Equipment

  • High-quality cameras (DSLR or mirrorless)
  • Lenses (various focal lengths)
  • Tripods and stabilizers
  • Microphones (lapel, shotgun, podcasting)
  • Drones for aerial footage
  • Lighting equipment
  • Portable storage drives and SD cards

Office & Documentation Equipment

  • Laptops (in good working condition)
  • Printers and scanners
  • Projectors for community presentations
  • Whiteboards and stationery supplies

Transport Equipment

  • Bicycles for local travel
  • A reliable motorcycle for regional travel
  • A utility vehicle or truck for transporting materials

Plants & Seeds: The Foundation of Our Farms

We are creating resilient, diverse food forests. We are seeking non-GMO, open-pollinated seeds and saplings, particularly native and well-adapted varieties.

Types of Plants Needed

  • Fruit & Nut Trees (e.g., Mango, Litchi, Avocado, Macadamia)
  • Perennial Vegetables
  • Medicinal Herbs
  • Nitrogen-Fixing Plants
  • Bamboo varieties
  • Native and endangered plant species
