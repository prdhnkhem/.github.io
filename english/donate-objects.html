Donate Objects: Build Our Foundation

A regenerative world is built not just with funds, but with knowledge, biodiversity, and the right tools. This page is an invitation to contribute the very fabric of our future community hubs through tangible, meaningful gifts.

Build Our Library: A Call for Knowledge

Our vision is to create a rich, physical library at each Hunate Hub. These libraries will be vibrant centers of learning, open to our partner families, the children in the orphanages, local visitors, and international volunteers. They will be repositories of wisdom, holding knowledge on everything from permaculture and natural building to philosophy and spiritual science. Your donation of a single book can become a seed of inspiration that grows for generations.

See Our Full Book Wishlist

Our Wishlist Focus

We are seeking books in the following key categories to build a well-rounded collection:

  • Permaculture & Regenerative Agriculture
  • Natural Building & Eco-Architecture
  • Vedic Sciences (Astrology, Vastu, Ayurveda)
  • Eastern Philosophy & Spiritual Texts
  • Holistic Health & Herbalism
  • Practical Skills & Crafts
  • Inspiring Children's Books

Grow Our Seed Bank: A Call for Biodiversity

A resilient community begins with a resilient food system. We are on a critical mission to create a local, open-source seed bank at each Hunate Hub. This is more than just a collection of seeds; it is a living library of genetic diversity, a guarantee of food sovereignty for our communities, and a sacred act of preserving the ancient heirloom varieties that are disappearing from our world. Your donation of seeds is a direct investment in the health and security of future generations.

See Our Full Seed Wishlist

Our Seed Bank Focus

We are seeking open-pollinated, non-GMO, and heirloom seeds in these categories:

  • Heirloom & Native Vegetables
  • Medicinal & Culinary Herbs
  • Nitrogen-Fixing Plants
  • Dynamic Accumulators
  • Companion Flowers & Pollinator Plants

Equip Our Builders: A Call for Tools

As we begin the physical construction of our hubs, we will have a growing need for quality, durable tools. From hammers and saws for building our natural homes to shovels and hoes for cultivating our gardens, the right tools empower our volunteers and partners to work safely and effectively. If you are a craftsperson, a builder, or a company with quality tools to donate, your contribution would be a foundational gift to our mission.

Contact Us to Coordinate a Tool Donation

Gifts That Grow

These tangible donations—a book that inspires a new idea, a seed that feeds a family, a tool that builds a home—are not just objects. They are the very building blocks of the regenerative world we are creating, together.
