Hunate World:

A Global Vision

The principles of Hunate are not bound by geography. The ultimate vision is a global network of interconnected, self-reliant communities creating a thriving planetary civilization.

From a National Model to a Global Network

The work in Nepal is the first step, the pilot project. Hunate World is the long-term vision of taking the successful, proven model developed through the 7-7-7 Initiative and Hunate Nepal and adapting it to diverse cultures, climates, and communities across the globe. We believe that the core principles of self-reliance, community, and ecological harmony are universal human needs.

The goal is to create a decentralized, peer-to-peer network of Hunate communities. This will not be an empire directed from a central point, but a living ecosystem of sovereign, interconnected hubs, each sharing their unique wisdom and innovations for the benefit of all. A hub in the Amazon rainforest will have different solutions than one in the European Alps, but both will operate on the same core principles of the Hunate philosophy.

"We envision a world where every community has the knowledge and tools to create its own resilient future, and a network to share its successes with all of humanity."

The Pillars of a Global Hunate Civilization

Creating a thriving global network requires more than just building hubs. It requires building the connective tissue between them.

  1. An Open-Source University: The knowledge gathered from Hunate HQ and all global hubs will be curated into a constantly evolving, open-source digital university. This will provide free access to blueprints, courses, and case studies on regenerative living for anyone in the world.
  2. A Global Council of Elders: We envision a council composed of mentors and leaders from various Hunate communities around the world. This council will not be a governing body, but a wisdom council, helping to guide the ethical evolution of the movement.
  3. A Resource-Based Exchange: We will develop systems for communities to exchange skills, knowledge, and even surplus resources without relying on the conventional financial system. This fosters a global culture of collaboration over competition.
  4. Annual Global Convergence: A yearly event, hosted by a different Hunate Hub, where members from around the world can gather to share knowledge, celebrate successes, and strengthen the bonds of our global family.

The Journey of a Thousand Miles

The vision of Hunate World is vast, but it begins with a single step. Every success in Nepal, every family empowered, and every community strengthened is a foundational stone for this global future. By supporting our work today, you are helping to plant the seeds for a regenerative world for generations to come.
