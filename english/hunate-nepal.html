Hunate Nepal: A National Blueprint

Beyond the initial 7-7-7 Initiative, our vision extends to a comprehensive national strategy for a resilient, self-sufficient, and regenerative Nepal.

From Local Hubs to a National Network

The 7-7-7 Initiative is the seed. The vision for Hunate Nepal is the forest that grows from it. The goal is to expand beyond the initial 14 hubs, fostering a thriving, interconnected network of Hunate communities across every district of the nation. This is not a centralized, top-down plan, but a decentralized, grassroots movement designed to empower local communities from within.

As each hub matures, it will become a center of learning and a source of inspiration for its region. The successful practices, crop varieties, and community governance models developed in one hub can be shared and adapted by others, creating a dynamic and resilient national network that is constantly learning and evolving.

"A truly resilient nation is not one with a strong center, but one with a strong and interconnected network of self-reliant communities."

Key Strategies for National Transformation

The Hunate Nepal strategy is built on several key pillars that work in synergy to create lasting change:

  1. Education for a New Generation: We will work to integrate the principles of "Becoming Hunatian" and Hunateculture into local schools and educational programs, fostering a new generation of conscious, skilled, and ecologically literate citizens.
  2. A Regenerative Economy: By connecting Hunate Hubs, we can create a new, circular economy. Hubs can trade surplus goods, share resources, and create local enterprises based on regenerative products and services, reducing dependence on fragile national supply chains.
  3. Open-Source Knowledge Sharing: A central goal of Hunate Nepal is to create a comprehensive, open-source digital library of regenerative practices adapted specifically for the diverse climates and cultures of Nepal. This knowledge will be freely available to all.
  4. Policy and Advocacy: While our focus is grassroots, we will also engage in advocacy to promote policies that support community self-sufficiency, land regeneration, and holistic education at the national level.

The Phased Rollout

The national vision will unfold in phases, growing organically from the success of our foundational work:

Phase 1: Establish the 14 Hubs (2026-2028)

The primary focus is on the successful implementation of the 7-7-7 Initiative, creating strong, functional models in each province.

Phase 2: Network & Knowledge Sharing (2028-2030)

With the hubs established, we will focus on building connections between them and developing the digital and physical infrastructure for knowledge sharing.

Phase 3: Expansion & Integration (2030 Onwards)

We will actively support the creation of new hubs inspired by the original seven and work to integrate Hunate principles into broader educational and economic systems.

A Vision for the Future of Nepal

Hunate Nepal is a vision of a country that is not just sustainable, but truly regenerative. A nation that leads the world by example, showing that it is possible to create a future that is both technologically advanced and deeply connected to its ecological and cultural roots. This is our blueprint for a thriving Nepal.
