From the Front Page

The Man Behind the Movement

This movement was born not from theory, but from a life of intense experience. The journey from a remote Nepali village through addiction and imprisonment to becoming a school principal and social innovator forged an unbreakable conviction: that a better world is possible. This is a testament to the power of personal transformation to inspire collective change.

Our Broken System

The modern world is built on flawed foundations. Our education system prioritizes memorization over wisdom, our economy values profit over well-being, and our social structures foster division instead of unity. The Hunate philosophy begins with a fearless diagnosis of this systemic failure.
