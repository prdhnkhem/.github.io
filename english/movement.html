The Hunate Movement:

Building a New World, Together

A philosophy is only as powerful as the action it inspires. The Hunate Movement translates our vision into a series of concrete, scalable initiatives designed to create tangible change from the local to the global level.

The 7-7-7 Initiative

Grassroots Action in Nepal

This is our flagship mission and the immediate focus of our work. The plan is to establish a network of 7 Hunate Hubs across the 7 provinces of Nepal. Each hub will be a self-sustaining community that supports 7 families and 7 orphanages, creating a powerful, replicable model for national transformation based on resilience and self-reliance.

Learn About the Initiative

Hunate HQ

The Future Hub of Innovation

The long-term vision is to build a central Hunate Headquarters in Nepal. This will be more than an office; it will be a living, breathing model of a regenerative village. It will serve as a global center for learning, research, and innovation, where new ideas can be tested and the blueprint for Hunate communities can be perfected and shared with the world.

Explore the HQ Vision

Hunate Nepal

A National Blueprint

Beyond the initial 7-7-7 Initiative, the goal is to create a comprehensive national strategy for a regenerative Nepal. This involves developing educational programs, influencing policy, and fostering a network of Hunate Hubs across the country, creating a resilient and self-sufficient future for the entire nation.

See the National Plan

Hunate World

A Global Vision

The principles of Hunate are universal. The ultimate vision is a global network of interconnected, self-reliant communities, all sharing knowledge and resources to create a thriving planetary civilization. Hunate World is the long-term goal of taking the successful model from Nepal and adapting it to cultures and climates across the globe.
