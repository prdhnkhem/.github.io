Phase 5

Building The 7 Provinces

This is where the blueprint meets the ground. Our first tangible mission is to move from theory to reality by establishing a decentralized network of living, breathing examples of Hunateculture across the nation of Nepal.

The Heart of the Mission: Why Families and Orphanages?

The "7-7-7 Initiative" is the first mission of the Hunate Movement. Our goal is to partner with one family and one orphanage in each of Nepal's seven provinces. This strategy is deliberate and deeply philosophical in nature.

Why Families? The family is the root of society. When families are broken, stressed, and disconnected, society itself becomes sick. By helping a family redesign their life to be self-reliant, healthy, and purposeful, we heal the community from its source. We are looking for families who feel trapped by the failing system, who dream of returning to the land, and who want to build a sovereign and meaningful life for their children. The family hub becomes the living proof that this dream is possible.

Why Orphanages? Children who have lost their parents are often free from the deep conditioning of the current system. They represent a unique and powerful potential. By providing them with a stable, loving environment rooted in Hunateculture, we give them more than just food and shelter; we give them the practical skills, the emotional resilience, and the holistic worldview to become the next generation of conscious leaders and regenerative builders.

The Search for Our Co-Creators: Hub Partner Criteria

This is a sacred partnership. We are searching for partners who resonate deeply with the Hunate vision and are ready for a multi-year journey of transformation. The core criteria are:

  • Must have legal, loan-free access to a minimum of 2 Bigha of land.
  • Must be a family unit of around 5 members (joint families are a priority) or a registered orphanage.
  • Must have some practical farming experience.
  • The land must be within a reasonable distance of a major city (approx. 10km radius or 45-minute drive).
  • Must be willing to be a public "Hunateculture Hub Partner" and a center for learning in your community.
  • Must understand that this is a partnership of knowledge and support, not a financial grant.

Our Sacred Partnership: How We Build Together

This is a journey we walk side-by-side as co-creators. The process is designed to be thorough, transparent, and deeply collaborative.

Step 1: Application & Selection
The journey begins with an application. Our team, guided by local District Representatives, will conduct interviews to ensure a true alignment of hearts and minds. We are not looking for employees; we are looking for partners in purpose.

Step 2: The Design Phase
Once selected, our core team of Hunate Engineers—Vedic Astrologers, Vastu experts, Permaculture designers, and more—will work intimately with you. We will analyze your land, your family's unique nature, and your dreams to co-create a complete, customized Hunateculture Life Design. This is the master blueprint for your home, your farm, your health, and your family's ethical business.

Step 3: The Build & Training Phase
With the blueprint in hand, we build. The Hunate Movement will provide expert guidance, hands-on training from our Mentors, and a global network of skilled Volunteers to help you implement the design. We will teach you how to build your regenerative farm, launch your family business, and become a beacon of knowledge in your community.

Our Vow to You

The Hunate Provides:

  • A complete, personalized Life Design Blueprint.
  • A complete, personalized Home, Farm, Business, Community Design Blueprint.
  • Expert training and mentorship.
  • Access to a global network of volunteers and supporters.
  • A pathway to a sovereign, purposeful livelihood.
  • A complete manual for farming, running a business, social media, finance, and more

Your Vow to the Mission

The Hub Partner Provides:

  • The land, the heart, and the unwavering commitment.
  • A willingness to learn, grow, and de-condition.
  • A passion for serving your community.
  • The courage to become a living example of a new way.
  • Help back those who helped you build.

The Result: A Vision of Life in a Hunate Hub

Imagine a life of profound purpose. Your home, built from the earth, is a sanctuary of health. Your land is a vibrant, productive ecosystem that provides your family with nutrient-dense food and a sustainable income. Your children are not just learning from books; they are learning real-world skills, connected to nature, and growing up in a supportive, loving community. Your family is not just surviving; you are thriving. You are no longer just a farmer or a householder; you are a leader, a teacher, and a beacon of hope for your entire region. This is the tangible result of our sacred partnership.
