The Hunate Research System

Exploring Ways to Restore Balance and Harmony Among Humanity, Nature, and Technology. This is not a fixed ideology, but a living inquiry into a new way of being.

Why HUNATE Exists: The Search for Truth

Modern science and technology, meant to enlighten us, are often not truly free. Knowledge is capitalized, controlled by a few who hold the data and thus the power. While information is abundant online, it is over-polluted and confusing, leading to more mistrust than liberation. If you want to understand your own body, the effects of food, or how to farm, you are met with an overwhelming storm of conflicting claims. It shouldn't be this hard.

We have technologies to travel to space and print DNA, yet essential knowledge about how to live is not freely available. There is no one trusted place to look for truth. We've found that many ancient cultures already understood the human body and nature, but their wisdom is often lost in translation or hidden by modern economic systems. Therefore, as Hunate, we believe it is essential for every individual to become a researcher of their own life through:

  • Self-exploration
  • Self-observation
  • Self-reflection
  • Self-analysis
  • Self-experience
  • Self-intuition
  • Self-reliance
"Someone eating food won't fill your stomach and hunger. You must learn to be at peace yourself. You must find your own truth."

Our Vision: A World in Harmony

We envision a future where humans, nature, and technology do not merely coexist, but flourish together in profound harmony. It is a world where humanity embraces its identity as one interconnected family on a single planet, transcending all outdated divisions.

The Progressive Human

Cultivating our highest selves through continuous wisdom-seeking, holistic well-being, and a deep sense of global citizenship, recognizing Earth as one country.

Nature as Our Sacred Foundation

Actively restoring the Earth's health, treating it with reverence, and integrating its principles into every aspect of our design and living.

Technology as a Harmonizing Force

Developing and utilizing tools ethically to serve life, generate abundance, and facilitate global unity, transforming societal structures for the good of all.

Our Mission & Core Goals

Our mission is to explore, understand, and reveal new systems of living that fundamentally heal the disconnections between humans and their true nature, between nature and its sacred place in our lives, and between technology and its purpose to serve life. Our core goals are:

  1. Find the Truth from Past, History, and Myths: Uncover the hidden knowledge and erased wisdom of our ancestors.
  2. Reawaken Humanity: Empower individuals to become aware, conscious, multi-skilled, and self-reliant beings.
  3. Restore Nature: Regenerate ecosystems by redesigning human interaction with land, food, water, and air.
  4. Redesign Life, Society & Technology: Innovate tools and systems that aid healing, connection, and ecological balance.
  5. Redefine Success & Systems: Question and redesign failing systems (education, health, economy) into life-affirming alternatives.
  6. Share Knowledge Freely: Create and distribute open-source handbooks, courses, and tools for global transformation.
  7. Unify Across Cultures & Time: Merge ancestral wisdom, present challenges, and future visions into a living model of planetary citizenship.

The Journey: Phases of Development

Phase 1: Journey of Self-Discovery (1991-2019)

My personal life of curiosity, pain, healing, and self-questioning, which became the inspiration to seek solutions to the world's problems.

Phase 2: The Joon Project (2013-2023)

An experimental international hub to build a better community, testing alternative social systems based on nature and ancient knowledge.

Phase 3: Reflection & Planning (2023-2026)

A period of reflection to synthesize the lessons from the past and plan the concrete steps for the future.

Phase 4: The HUNATE Movement (2026 Onwards)

Implementing our findings in the real world, step-by-step, without protest or blame, to heal ourselves and the planet together.

Phase 5: HUNATE HQ (2028 Onwards)

Building a practical, purposeful, and regenerative community in Nepal to serve as a living model for the world.

The first journey for you is to become a "Hunatian" by rewriting your thought patterns and changing your habits. From there, we can change the world.
