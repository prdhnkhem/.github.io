Our Community of Co-Creators

The Hunate Movement is not built by one person, but by a diverse, global tribe of individuals who have answered the call to build a better future. This is a community of purpose, not a corporate hierarchy.

The Core Team: The Engine Room

The Engineers

The designers of the blueprint. This team of experts in Vedic science, permaculture, and modern tech creates the customized Hunateculture plans for our Hub Partners.

Learn More

The Mentors

The keepers of wisdom. These are seasoned masters in their respective fields who guide our Engineers and ensure the integrity and depth of the Hunate philosophy.

Learn More

The Admins

The guardians of the hub. This vital team manages the daily operations, logistics, and support systems that allow the entire community to function smoothly and effectively.

Learn More

The Community Network: The On-the-Ground Force

The Hub Partners

The beacons of the new world. These are the families and orphanages who partner with us, providing the land and heart to create the first living examples of Hunateculture.

Learn More

The District Reps

The roots of the movement. These trusted local leaders are our eyes and ears on the ground, helping us find and support Hub Partners in every district of Nepal.

Learn More

The Volunteers

The hands of the movement. Skilled builders, gardeners, and creatives from around the world who join our hubs to contribute their energy and learn new skills.
