The Hunate Community Seed Bank

We are on a critical mission to create a resilient, local, open-source seed bank at each Hunate Hub. This is a living library of genetic diversity, a guarantee of food sovereignty for our communities, and a sacred act of preserving our ancestral heirloom varieties.

Vegetables (तरकारी)

Plant Name (English / Nepali) Variety / Use
Tomato / गोलभेंडा Heirloom varieties for sauce, slicing, and drying
Cucumber / काँक्रो Pickling and fresh-eating varieties
Chili / खुर्सानी Local hot varieties (Dalle, Akabare) and mild varieties
Spinach / पालुङ्गो Heat-tolerant and cold-hardy varieties
Beans / सिमी Bush beans, pole beans, and drying beans

Medicinal Herbs (औषधीय जडिबुटी)

Plant Name (English / Nepali) Variety / Use
Tulsi / Holy Basil (तुलसी) For tea and medicinal use
Titepati / Mugwort (तितेपाती) Medicinal and insect-repellent properties
Ashwagandha (अश्वगन्धा) Adaptogenic root for stress relief

Flowers (फूल)

Plant Name (English / Nepali) Variety / Use
Marigold / सयपत्री For pest control in gardens and cultural use
Gomphrena / मखमली For cultural use and as a beautiful border plant

How to Contribute Seeds

We are seeking open-pollinated, non-GMO, and heirloom varieties to build a resilient and diverse seed collection. If you have seeds to share from your own garden or from a trusted, ethical supplier, please contact us to coordinate. Your gift of biodiversity is an investment in the future.
