Get Involved: Find Your Role in the Hunate Movement

The Hunate Movement is a collective effort. It requires a diverse team of passionate individuals to bring its vision to life. This is your invitation to find the role that best fits your skills and passion, and to join us in building a new world.

Hunate Engineers

Role: Hunate Engineers are the technical architects and builders of our vision. They are the experts who design and implement the physical and digital systems for our Hunate Hubs.

Requirements: Expertise in fields such as permaculture, eco-architecture, renewable energy, water management, web development, or other relevant technical domains. A commitment to open-source principles and a desire to create sustainable, practical solutions.

Apply as an Engineer

Hunate Mentors

Role: Hunate Mentors are the wisdom-keepers and guides of the movement. They provide the educational and spiritual foundation for our communities, guiding individuals on their journey of "Becoming Hunatian."

Requirements: Deep knowledge and practical experience in fields such as yoga, meditation, Ayurveda, Vedic astrology, holistic health, non-violent communication, or other practices that support personal transformation.

Apply as a Mentor

Hunate Donors

Role: Hunate Donors are the essential partners who provide the financial fuel for our movement. Their contributions are direct investments in building a new world, funding land acquisition, infrastructure, and the establishment of Hunate Hubs.

Requirements: A desire to support a regenerative future and the financial capacity to contribute. We value transparency and will provide clear records of how all funds are utilized.

Record Your Donation

Hunate Volunteers

Role: Hunate Volunteers are the hands and hearts of the movement. They contribute their time, energy, and passion to the practical, on-the-ground work of building our communities.

Requirements: A strong work ethic, a positive attitude, and a willingness to learn and contribute to a variety of tasks, from farming and construction to cooking and community outreach. No specific expertise is required, only a desire to serve.

Apply as a Volunteer

Hunate District Representatives

Role: District Representatives are our local ambassadors and community organizers. They act as the bridge between the central Hunate Movement and their local district, helping to identify partners, spread awareness, and build a grassroots network.

Requirements: Strong communication and organizational skills, a deep connection to their local community, and a passionate belief in the Hunate vision.

Apply as a District Rep

Hunate Hub Partners

Role: Hub Partners are the pioneering families, groups, or organizations who will establish and run the Hunate Hubs. They are the core of the 7-7-7 Initiative, dedicating their land and lives to creating a living model of the Hunate philosophy.

Requirements: Own or have long-term access to land, have a committed team, and share a deep alignment with the Hunate vision. See the 7-7-7 Initiative page for full details.

Apply as a Hub Partner
