Get Involved: Find Your Role in the Hunate Movement

The Hunate Movement is a collective effort. It requires a diverse team of passionate individuals to bring its vision to life. This is your invitation to find the role that best fits your skills and passion, and to join us in building a new world.

Join as a Hunate Engineer

Hunate Engineers are the technical architects of our vision. You are the designers, builders, and innovators who will create the physical and digital infrastructure for a regenerative future. Your role is to translate the Hunate philosophy into practical, sustainable, and beautiful systems that can be replicated across our network of hubs.

What We're Looking For:

We need experts with a deep commitment to open-source principles and a passion for creating life-affirming technologies. Your expertise could be in:

  • Permaculture Design: Creating self-sufficient, edible landscapes and closed-loop systems.
  • Eco-Architecture: Designing and building structures with natural materials like earthbags, bamboo, and reclaimed wood.
  • Renewable Energy Systems: Implementing off-grid solar, wind, or micro-hydro solutions.
  • Water Management: Designing systems for rainwater harvesting, greywater recycling, and natural filtration.
  • Web & IT: Building the open-source digital platforms that will connect our global community and share our knowledge freely.
  • Mechanical Systems: Innovating appropriate technologies for daily life.
Apply as an Engineer

Join as a Hunate Mentor

Mentors are the wisdom-keepers of the movement. Your role is to guide individuals and communities through the process of personal transformation, providing the educational and spiritual foundation for a new way of living.

Apply as a Mentor

Join as a District Representative

District Representatives are our local ambassadors. You are the bridge between the Hunate Movement and your local community, helping to build a grassroots network of support, awareness, and action.

Apply as a District Rep

Join as a Hunate Hub Partner

This is the most crucial role in the 7-7-7 Initiative. Hunate Hub Partners are the pioneering families, groups, or organizations who will establish and run the Hunate Hubs. You are the core of the movement, dedicating your land and lives to creating a living, breathing model of the Hunate philosophy for your entire province to see.

Requirements for Partnership:

  1. Land & Vision: You must own or have secure, long-term access to a piece of land that can support a small community and agricultural activities. You must share our vision for creating a regenerative hub.
  2. A Committed Team: You are not alone. You must have a core team or family who is committed to living and working on the project, ready to embrace the challenges and rewards of community building.
  3. Philosophical Alignment: A deep resonance with the Hunate principles of self-reliance, ecological harmony, and personal transformation is essential. This is a life path, not just a project.

As a Hub Partner, you will work directly with our team of Mentors and Engineers to co-design and build your hub. This is a deep, collaborative partnership to create a beacon of hope for your community.

Apply as a Hub Partner
