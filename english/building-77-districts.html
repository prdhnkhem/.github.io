Phase 7: Building The 77 Districts

Following the success of the 7-province model, this phase represents the national scaling of the Hunate Movement, transforming our proven hubs into a resilient, nationwide network of light and learning.

From a Network of Hubs to a Regenerative Nation

The goal of Phase 7 is to establish at least one Hunate Hub in every one of Nepal's 77 districts. This is how we move from a series of successful experiments to creating lasting, systemic change at the national level. The strategy for this expansion is organic and self-replicating. The original hubs from Phase 5 will become the official Hunateculture training centers for their respective regions. The experienced families and teams who have walked the path will become the mentors for the next wave of Hub Partners.

This creates a powerful, decentralized system of knowledge transfer, where wisdom is passed from neighbor to neighbor, from community to community. The impact of this phase will be the creation of a "Hunate Nepal"—a nation with true local food security, revitalized rural economies, and a global reputation as a destination for learning the art and science of regenerative living. It is a vision of a nation that is not just sustainable, but thriving, beautiful, and deeply connected to its roots.

Building a Nation Starts with a Seed

A regenerative Nepal is not a distant dream; it is the destination at the end of a clear path. By joining the movement today, you help us plant the first seven seeds that will grow into this national forest of transformation.
