Support Our Engineers: Invest in the Tools of Change

The Hunate vision is built on a foundation of practical, tangible solutions. Our Engineers are the technical experts who turn philosophy into function. Your support provides them with the essential resources they need to design and build a regenerative future.

Why Support Our Engineers?

Hunate Engineers are the architects, permaculturists, and innovators creating the systems for our Hunate Hubs. They design the eco-buildings, implement the renewable energy systems, and build the open-source software that will connect our communities. Supporting them is a direct investment in the core infrastructure of the movement.

Where Your Contribution Goes

Funds designated for Hunate Engineers are used to provide the vital resources that make their work possible. This includes:

  • Electronics: Laptops, cameras, storage drives, and other hardware for design, documentation, and communication.
  • Office Equipment & Stationery: Basic supplies for planning, drafting, and managing projects.
  • Travel & Site Visits: Fuel and transportation costs for engineers to visit and collaborate with Hub Partners across Nepal.
  • Food & Accommodation: Basic living stipends for full-time engineers dedicating their lives to the project.
  • Fun & Celebration: Resources to foster a healthy, joyful, and collaborative team culture.
  • Gifts: Small tokens of appreciation for the immense value and expertise our engineers contribute.
  • Educational Growth: Funds for training, workshops, and courses to ensure our team is always learning and growing.
