The Engineers: Designers of the Blueprint

The Hunate Engineers are the master craftspeople of our movement. They are the core team of experts who weave together diverse fields of ancient wisdom and modern science to create the holistic, personalized Hunateculture life designs for our Hub Partners and the world.

Our Integrated Team: The Roles We Are Calling

Astrologer (Jyotishi)

Guides our team and partner families with auspicious timing according to Vedic astrology. Helps design life guidance courses, create educational content, and map the cosmic DNA for personalized life designs.

Vastu Consultant

Helps choose land, design homes and farms in harmony with Vastu Shastra, perform land purification rituals, and create video courses to teach the science of sacred architecture.

Ayurveda Expert

Helps families understand their unique mind-body constitution (dosha), designs personalized diets, establishes herbal gardens, and provides knowledge on home remedies and holistic health.

Permaculture Designer

Designs the entire agricultural ecosystem of our hubs, including water systems, food forests, animal integration, and waste management, while training the community in these skills.

Renewable Energy Expert

Designs and implements the off-grid energy systems for our hubs, including solar power, biogas digesters, and other sustainable energy solutions.

Natural Builder / Eco-Architect

Leads the design and construction of our buildings, using local, earth-friendly materials like cob, adobe, bamboo, and stone to create healthy, breathable, and beautiful homes.

Water Expert

Focuses on the complete water cycle of a hub, designing systems for rainwater harvesting, greywater recycling, wastewater treatment, and natural pool filtration.

Yoga & Meditation Teacher

Leads the community in daily Sadhana (spiritual practice), providing the inner foundation of mindfulness, presence, and equanimity that is essential for this work.

Healers

Experts in various healing modalities (e.g., sound healing, bodywork, herbalism) who support the physical, emotional, and spiritual well-being of the community.

Ethical Technologists (IT, AI, Automation)

Builds the digital nervous system of the project, creating software for data management, automating farm systems, and connecting our global community through ethical technology.

Researchers & Scientists

Documents the entire Hunateculture process, measuring key metrics related to soil health, human well-being, and economic output to scientifically validate our model.

Artists, Musicians, Storytellers

The cultural heart of the community. This team weaves beauty, meaning, and joy into the fabric of daily life through art, music, ceremony, and storytelling.
