Support Our Hub Partners: Directly Build the Future

Contributing to our Hub Partners is the most direct and impactful way to support the Hunate Movement. You are providing the foundational resources for families to build living, breathing examples of regenerative communities.

Why Support Our Hub Partners?

The Hunate Hub Partners are the heart of the 7-7-7 Initiative. They are the families and groups on the ground, dedicating their land and lives to creating the future we envision. Your support empowers them to transform their land into productive, self-sufficient, and educational centers that will serve their families, local orphanages, and the entire community.

Where Your Contribution Goes

Funds designated for Hub Partners are used for the essential capital investments needed to establish a thriving, self-reliant homestead. This is a direct investment in tangible, life-sustaining assets:

  • Plants and Seeds: Acquiring a diverse range of fruit trees, medicinal herbs, and heirloom seeds to establish a food forest.
  • Book: Creating Library for community rich with knowledge and wisdoms.
  • Kitchen Setups: Equipping the community kitchen with efficient stoves, food preservation tools, and other essentials.
  • Construction Tools & Materials: Purchasing materials for building natural homes, workshops, and community spaces.
  • Farming Tools & Equipment: Acquiring high-quality hand tools and appropriate technology for regenerative agriculture.
  • Greenhouse & Nursery: Constructing protected spaces to propagate plants and extend the growing season.
  • Pond Construction: Excavating ponds for water storage, aquaculture, and creating a resilient water system.
  • Animals and Shelters: Building appropriate housing for small livestock that will be integrated into the farm ecosystem.
  • Business Startup: Seed funding for small, on-site enterprises (e.g., selling produce, crafts, or workshops) to ensure long-term financial self-sufficiency.
  • Travel, Training & Education: Enabling partners to attend workshops, trainings, and visit other successful projects to continuously learn and grow.
