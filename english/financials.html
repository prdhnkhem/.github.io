Financials: A Commitment to Transparency

A movement built on trust must be built on transparency. This page is the home for our commitment to financial honesty, now and into the future. We are accountable to our community, not to outside interests.

Why We Share Our Financials

The Hunate Movement is a community-funded initiative, independent of corrupt or compromising systems. We are powered by the trust and contributions of individuals from around the world who believe in our mission. Because our support comes directly from you, we have a sacred responsibility to be open and honest about how every contribution is used. Transparency is not an afterthought; it is a core principle. It is our promise to you that your support is directly and efficiently channeled into the work of building a regenerative world.

The Future of This Page

The Hunate Movement is currently in its foundational stages (Phase 4: building the team and finding our first partners). As such, detailed financial reports are not yet available. However, as the project grows and our first hubs are established, this page will become the central hub for our financial reporting. You can expect to find:

  • Quarterly Financial Summaries: Simple, easy-to-understand reports on our income and expenditures.
  • Project-Specific Funding Goals: Clear, transparent goals for major milestones, like acquiring land for the Hunate HQ or funding a new hub.
  • Impact Reports: Demonstrating how community contributions have translated into tangible results on the groundâ€”from tools purchased to hubs built.
