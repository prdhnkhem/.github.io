The 7-7-7 Initiative

A grassroots blueprint for national transformation, starting now. This is our flagship mission to create a powerful, replicable model for a self-reliant and regenerative Nepal.

Mission and Vision

The 7-7-7 Initiative is the first, tangible phase of the Hunate Movement. It is a focused, practical plan with a clear and powerful goal: to establish 14 Hunate Hubs across the 7 Provinces of Nepal. Each of these hubs will be a self-sustaining community designed to directly support and empower 7 local families and 7 local orphanages.

This is not a charity project; it is a strategic initiative to build a decentralized network of resilience. By creating these living models of Hunateculture, we aim to prove that a regenerative, community-focused way of life is not only possible but scalable. Each hub will become a lighthouse for its province‚Äîa center for learning, innovation, and hope that can inspire and guide the surrounding communities.

"We are not waiting for the system to change. We are building the new system, one community at a time."

Partnership Hub: Process & Criteria

The success of this initiative depends on finding the right partners on the ground. We are looking for families, groups, or organizations who are ready to dedicate their land, time, and energy to becoming a Hunate Hub. The process is one of deep collaboration.

  1. Application: Interested parties must fill out the Hunate Hub Partners application form, providing details about their land, team, and vision.
  2. Selection: We will carefully review all applications based on alignment with Hunate principles, team commitment, and the potential for local impact.
  3. Collaboration: Selected partners will work closely with our team of Hunate Mentors and Engineers to design and implement the Hunateculture system for their specific location and needs.

Who Should Apply?

We are seeking partners who meet the following criteria:

  • Own or have long-term access to a suitable piece of land.
  • Have a committed team or family ready to live and work on the project.
  • Share a deep alignment with the Hunate philosophy of self-reliance and ecological harmony.
  • Have a strong desire to serve their local community and share their knowledge openly.
  • Have land around 10 KM around the City

The Team We Need

This is a collective effort that requires a diverse team of passionate individuals. We are calling for skilled and dedicated people to fill the following core roles. This is your invitation to contribute your unique talents to building a new world.

Hunate Engineers

The architects and builders of our vision. We need experts in astrology, ayurveda, vastu shastra, yoga, permaculture, eco-architecture, renewable energy, web development, lawyer, and other technical fields to design and build the physical and digital infrastructure of the hubs.

Hunate Mentors

The guides and wisdom-keepers. Same as engineers, we are looking for experts in yoga, meditation, Ayurveda, Vedic astrology, and other holistic practices to guide the personal and spiritual development of our hub communities.

Hunate Donors

The fuel for our movement. Financial supporters are crucial partners who provide the resources needed to acquire land, build infrastructure, and fund the initial setup of our Hunate Hubs.

Hunate Volunteers

The hands and hearts of the project. We need passionate individuals to contribute their time and energy on the ground, helping with everything from construction and farming to community outreach and documentation.

Hunate District Representatives

Our local ambassadors. We need dedicated individuals in each district to act as a bridge between the Hunate Movement and the local community, helping to identify partners, organize events, and spread the vision.
