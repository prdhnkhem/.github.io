Support Hunate HQ: Invest in the Heart of the Movement

The Hunate Headquarters is our long-term vision for a global center for learning and innovation. Contributions to this fund are a direct investment in the permanent, physical home for the Hunate philosophy.

Why Support the Hunate HQ?

While our current work focuses on establishing a decentralized network of hubs, the long-term vision includes a central lighthouse‚Äîa place that can serve as a world-class educational facility and a living proof-of-concept for Hunateculture. Supporting the HQ fund is an investment in the future legacy of this movement, ensuring it has a home for generations to come.

Where Your Contribution Goes

The Hunate HQ fund is a dedicated, long-term savings fund that will be used for every aspect of creating this pioneering community from the ground up. Your support will be allocated across these critical areas:

Foundational & Land

  • Land Acquisition: Securing a large, suitable piece of land in Nepal to house the entire community.
  • Legal & Operations: Covering legal fees, registration, and initial operational costs.
  • Web Hosting & Publication: Maintaining the digital infrastructure to share our progress and knowledge.

Construction & Infrastructure

  • Building Materials & Tools: Purchasing natural, reclaimed, and essential materials for all construction.
  • Volunteer & Guest Houses: Building beautiful, natural homes to host our team and visitors.
  • Orphanage Buildings: Creating a safe, nurturing, and empowering home for children at the heart of the community.
  • Workshops & Classrooms: Constructing dedicated spaces for learning, innovation, and skill-sharing.

Farm & Food Systems

  • Farm Development: Earthworks, pond construction, and creating the initial layout for a productive ecosystem.
  • Farming Tools & Equipment: Acquiring high-quality tools for regenerative agriculture.
  • Plants and Seeds: Establishing a diverse food forest with fruit trees, medicinal herbs, and a seed bank.

Community & Education

  • Library & Teaching Materials: Stocking our community library with essential books and creating educational resources.
  • Electronics & Office: Equipping our office and media lab with computers, cameras, and other tools for documentation and global outreach.
