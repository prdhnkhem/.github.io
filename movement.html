The Hunate Movement

A citizen-led initiative to stop talking about a better future and start building it, one family, one village, one act of courage at a time.

Our First Mission: The 7-7-7 Initiative

This is not a theoretical exercise. This is our on-the-ground, multi-year mission to create living proof that a regenerative future is possible. We are planting 14 seeds of change across the entire nation of Nepal.

The Plan is Simple and Bold:

  • We will partner with 7 dedicated families who have land and a deep desire for a self-sufficient life.
  • We will partner with 7 forward-thinking orphanages to help them become true homes of healing, growth, and resilience.
  • We will establish these 14 "Hunateculture Hubs" across the 7 provinces of Nepal.

Over a five-year period, our team of Hunate Engineers will guide each hub through a complete life redesign. They will become lighthouses in their communities—fully self-reliant in food, water, and energy, operating ethical family businesses, and serving as living classrooms for their neighbors to learn from.

The First Step: Becoming Hunatian

Global change begins with individual transformation. Before we can redesign the world, we must redesign ourselves. "Becoming Hunatian" is our free, 3-month public course—a self-guided journey to reclaim your life from the failing system.

A Journey of Self-Mastery

This is not a typical online course. It is a series of practical tasks and profound reflections designed to help you:

  • Detox Your Mind: Learn to observe your thoughts, break free from conditioning, and find your own inner voice.
  • Heal Your Body: Reconnect with your physical self through simple exercises, mindful eating, and natural living, free from chemicals.
  • Filter Your World: Make conscious choices about the media you consume, the products you use, and the relationships you nurture.
  • Find Your True Purpose: Move beyond the fake dreams implanted by society and discover a purpose rooted in your own truth.

The Architects of Change

This movement is powered by a dedicated, multi-skilled family of doers, thinkers, and guardians. Each role is a vital part of the whole ecosystem.

Hunate Engineers

The life designers. A core team of young, multi-skilled individuals who guide our partner hubs. They are the architects, permaculturists, astrologers, and wellness guides who translate the Hunateculture blueprint into reality on the ground.

Hunate Mentors

The guardians of wisdom. Respected elders and professionals from each field who provide ethical guidance, share their deep experience, and ensure the integrity of our work. They are our compass.

Hunate Hubs

The pioneers. The families and orphanages who partner with us. They are not clients; they are our co-creators. Their homes and their lives become the living, breathing proof that this vision works.

This is a living movement, and it needs you.

Whether you have the skills to be an Engineer, the wisdom to be a Mentor, or the courage to become a Hub, there is a place for you here.
