The Blueprint: The HUNATE Movement

This is where philosophy meets the soil. The HUNATE Movement is the work of my life—a citizen-led, open-source initiative to build a future that is regenerative by design.

On this page:

  • I. The First Mission
  • II. The Architects of Change
  • III. The Educational Foundation

I. The First Mission: The 7-7-7 Initiative

A vision is only as powerful as the action it inspires. My first mission is a direct, on-the-ground plan to plant 14 seeds of change across Nepal, creating living proof that a better world is possible. This is not a theoretical exercise; it is a five-year commitment to tangible transformation.

The plan is simple and profound:

  • I will partner with 7 brave families and 7 forward-thinking orphanages.
  • We will establish these 14 "Hunateculture Hubs" across the 7 provinces of Nepal.

Over five years, my team and I will guide each hub through a complete life redesign using the principles of Hunateculture. They will become lighthouses in their communities—fully self-reliant in food, health, and energy, operating ethical businesses, and serving as real-world classrooms for others to learn from. This is not charity; this is a shared investment in human potential and ecological regeneration.

II. The Architects of Change: Our Team Structure

This work is too vast for one person. It requires a dedicated family of visionaries, builders, and guardians. I am gathering a team that embodies the multi-skilled, self-reliant spirit of the Hunatian, structured to ensure both visionary integrity and practical wisdom.

"We are not hiring employees. We are gathering co-creators. This is a call to those who seek not a career, but a calling."

The movement is built around two core groups:

  • Hunate Engineers: A core team of young, multi-skilled "life designers"—architects, permaculturists, astrologers, healers, and tech experts—who will work directly with our partner hubs to implement the Hunateculture blueprint.
  • Hunate Mentors: The guardians of wisdom. Respected elders and professionals who will provide ethical guidance and deep experience to my Engineers, ensuring our work is grounded in integrity and truth.

III. The Educational Foundation: "Becoming Hunatian"

Before one can join my core team or truly understand this work, one must do the inner work. I have designed "Becoming Hunatian," a free, 3-month public course for anyone who wants to begin this journey of self-transformation. It is the mandatory first step for any potential Engineer and the foundational text for the entire movement.

It is a path to unlearn the conditioning of the failing system and to reclaim your own health, purpose, and inner wisdom. The course is my open-source gift to anyone with the courage to begin.
