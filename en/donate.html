The Catalog of Impact

This is not a catalog of our needs; it is a map of our shared dreams. The Hunate Project is built on a simple, powerful truth: a better world is not only possible, it is waiting to be grown. Every contribution is a seed you plant with us for a regenerative future. We operate with radical transparency, and every rupee is tracked publicly. Your trust is our most sacred vow.

Where Your Support Goes

  • Nurturing a Hunate Hub: Your support provides the tangible seeds, tools, and materials for a family or orphanage to build a thriving, self-sufficient home. This includes everything from a food forest starter kit to the foundations of a safe, natural house.
  • Empowering Our Engineers: Contributions help cover the modest travel, food, and accommodation costs for our dedicated core team, allowing them to provide essential on-the-ground guidance to our partner hubs across Nepal.
  • In-Kind Sponsorship: We also welcome in-kind donations of farming supplies, building materials, tools, and technology. If you can provide physical resources, you become a vital partner in our mission.
