Where Your Support Goes

You can choose to support a specific part of our mission. Your donation directly funds the tangible tools and resources that build self-sufficient communities.

Nurturing a Hunate Hub

  • • **A Food Forest Starter Kit:** Fund a diverse collection of fruit and nut tree saplings.
  • • **Tools of Self-Reliance:** Equip a family with a complete set of durable farming tools.
  • • **Water Security:** Fund the construction of a small pond for rainwater harvesting.
  • • **A Healthy Home:** Contribute to safe, non-toxic kitchen setups and natural building materials.

Empowering Our Engineers

  • • **Tools for Design:** Contribute to essential technology like laptops and cameras for our team.
  • • **The Journey of Service:** Fund an Engineer's travel to a remote hub to provide on-the-ground guidance.
  • • **Fuel for the Fire:** Support the basic food and accommodation costs for a full-time Engineer.

In-Kind Sponsorship

If you or your company can provide physical materials, you become a vital in-kind sponsor. We are always in need of:

  • • **Farming Supplies:** Quality seeds, fruit saplings, irrigation pipes, water pumps.
  • • **Building Materials:** Wood, bamboo, stone, and other natural construction materials.
  • • **Tools:** Carpentry tools, farming tools, safety equipment.
  • • **Technology:** Solar panels, batteries, laptops, cameras.

To become a sponsor, please get in touch via our contact page.
