About The Project

Project HUNATE was born from a deep inquiry into the failing systems of our world. We are a citizen-led movement in Nepal dedicated to researching, exploring, and sharing ways to create balance among Humanity, Nature, and Technology. We are not an NGO or a political party; we are a community of doers, dreamers, and designers building a regenerative future from the ground up.

Discover Our Story

Problems We See In The World

Our human-made systems—in health, education, economics, and governance—are broken, leading to disconnection and destruction. We see a world in crisis, built upon a profit-based economy that creates scarcity, greed, hunger, and fear. This is a call to reflect on the systems we support and to seek a new path.

Our Initiatives

Our Finding: Hunateculture

A practical life design system that blends ancient wisdom with modern, ethical tools to create self-sufficient and harmonious lives, homes, and communities.

Our Process: The 7-7-7 Initiative

Our first mission to help 7 families and 7 orphanages across Nepal's 7 provinces become living examples of a thriving, regenerative future.

Our Goal: Hunate HQ

The long-term vision to build a physical base in Nepal. This HQ will serve as a "School of Life" and a living model of a regenerative community.
