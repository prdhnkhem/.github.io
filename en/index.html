About The Movement

A Journey to Restore Balance

Project HUNATE was born from a deep inquiry into the failing systems of our world. We are a citizen-led movement in Nepal dedicated to researching, exploring, and sharing ways to create balance among **Hu**manity, **Na**ture, and **Te**chnology. We are not an NGO or a political party; we are a community of doers, dreamers, and designers building a regenerative future from the ground up.

Our Initiatives

Our Finding: Hunateculture

The practical solution born from our research. It is a life design system that blends ancient wisdom with modern, ethical tools to create self-sufficient and harmonious lives, homes, and communities.

Details

Our Process: The 7-7-7 Initiative

Our first mission to help 7 families and 7 orphanages across Nepal's 7 provinces. We will guide them in redesigning their lives to become living examples of a thriving, regenerative future.

Details

Our Goal: Hunate HQ

The long-term vision is to build a physical base in Nepal. This headquarters will serve as a "School of Life," a research center, and a living, breathing model of a practical, regenerative community.
