Hunate Project Explanation

The Hunate Project is a research and development initiative dedicated to restoring balance between Humanity, Nature, and Technology. It serves as the overarching framework for all our activities. If Hunateculture is the design system, the Hunate Project is the entire operational blueprint that brings it to life. It is our structured approach to identifying the world's failing systems and implementing practical, regenerative solutions.

Becoming Hunatian Course

Global change begins with individual awakening. The "Journey to Becoming Hunatian" is a free, public, self-designed course that outlines the path of personal transformation. It is a structured curriculum with practical exercises to help you cultivate self-awareness, question societal conditioning, and rediscover your true purpose.

This world needs humans who see the problems, who are willing to find answers, and who can sacrifice selfish desires for a better future. This journey is designed to make sure you won't be easily manipulated or controlled by the failing social system. It is the foundational step for anyone wishing to engage with our work more deeply.

The Hunate Movement

The Hunate Movement is the public, citizen-led campaign to spread the Hunate philosophy and implement Hunateculture. It is the active, on-the-ground expression of our vision. This is the phase of active outreach, inviting individuals, families, and organizations to join the collective effort to redesign society from the ground up, with minimal reliance on traditional monetary or political structures.

We want to share our stories, our findings, and the Hunateculture Life Design System with everyone. We aim to spark curiosity, open minds, and inspire a mass movement of citizens working together for change.

Our First Project: The 7-7-7 Initiative

The 7-7-7 Initiative is our first pilot project where the Hunate Project is helping rethink, redesign, and rebuild life, family, farm, and business by applying Hunateculture Principles. Our goal is to help 7 families and 7 orphanages across the 7 different provinces of Nepal to live according to Hunateculture.

These partners will function as Hunateculture Hubs, becoming learning centers for their communities and tangible proof that this new way of life is not just a dream, but a practical reality.
