Our Phased Approach

The Hunate Movement is a long-term vision that unfolds in carefully planned phases. Each phase builds upon the last, moving from personal discovery to community action and, eventually, global expansion.

Phase 1 & 2: Discovery & Experimentation (1991-2023)

[cite_start]This was the period of self-discovery and the experimental "The Joon Project," where the core ideas of Hunate were forged through travel, volunteering, and building a prototype international community[cite: 215, 215, 837].

Phase 3: Planning & Documentation (2023-2026)

The current phase of reflection and meticulous planning. [cite_start]We are documenting our learnings, refining our systems, and building the complete architectural blueprint for the Hunate Movement before public launch[cite: 216, 1005].

Phase 4: The Hunate Movement (2026 Onwards)

The public execution phase. [cite_start]This involves launching our awareness campaign, recruiting our core team, and beginning the on-the-ground work of the 7-7-7 Initiative[cite: 216, 1015].

Phase 5: Hunate HQ (2028 Onwards)

The establishment of our physical base in Nepal. [cite_start]The Hunate HQ will be a living model of a regenerative community, a "School of Life," and an operational center for the movement[cite: 61, 217].
