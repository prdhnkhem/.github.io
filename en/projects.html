The Hunate Project

The Blueprint for a New World

The Hunate Project is a research and development initiative dedicated to restoring balance between Humanity, Nature, and Technology. It serves as the overarching framework for all our activities. If Hunateculture is the design system, the Hunate Project is the entire operational blueprint that brings it to life. It is our structured approach to identifying problems and implementing solutions.

Becoming Hunatian: The First Step

A Journey to Reclaim Your Life

Global change begins with individual awakening. The "Journey to Becoming Hunatian" is a free, public, self-designed course that outlines the path of personal transformation. It is a structured curriculum with practical exercises to help you cultivate self-awareness, question societal conditioning, and rediscover your true purpose.

This is the foundational step for anyone wishing to engage with our work more deeply. It equips you with the inner resilience and clarity needed to become a self-reliant, conscious being ready to contribute to a regenerative future.

The Hunate Movement

A Call to Collective Action

The Hunate Movement is the public, citizen-led campaign to spread the Hunate philosophy and implement Hunateculture. It is the active, on-the-ground expression of our vision, starting in 2026. This is the phase of active outreach, inviting individuals, families, and organizations to join the collective effort to redesign society from the ground up, with minimal reliance on traditional monetary or political structures.

Our First Project: The 7-7-7 Initiative

Creating Living Proof

The 7-7-7 Initiative is our first pilot project where the Hunate Project is helping rethink, redesign, and rebuild life, family, farm, and business by applying Hunateculture Principles. Our goal is to help **7 families and 7 orphanages** across the **7 different provinces of Nepal** to live according to Hunateculture.

These partners will function as Hunateculture Hubs, becoming learning centers for their communities and tangible proof that this new way of life is not just a dream, but a practical reality.
