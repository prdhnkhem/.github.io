Our Finding: A New Way to Live

Hunateculture: The Design System

After years of research and experimentation, we arrived at a solution: **Hunateculture**. It is a practical life design system that transforms the HUNATE philosophy into real, everyday living. It blends ancient wisdom (like Vedic Astrology, Vastu, and Ayurveda) with modern, ethical approaches (like permaculture and eco-architecture) to create personalized lifestyles that foster balance and harmony.

The journey begins from within. Our public course, **"Becoming Hunatian,"** is a self-designed path for individuals to cultivate self-awareness, question conditioning, and align their lives with Hunate principles. This inner work is the foundation for all external change.

Our Process: The Hunate Movement

The Hunate Movement is our process for bringing Hunateculture to the community. It is a citizen-led initiative designed to awaken communities to the failings of current systems and share practical, on-the-ground solutions through a series of phased, strategic actions.

The 7-7-7 Initiative

Our first pilot project is to help **7 families and 7 orphanages** across the **7 provinces of Nepal** redesign their lives. This is our primary mission to create tangible proof that our model works.
