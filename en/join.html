Get Involved

This movement is a living ecosystem—a network of sovereign individuals united by a shared purpose. We are calling for co-creators to fill several vital roles. This is not a job application; it is an invitation to a path of service, growth, and profound impact.

Become a Hunate Engineer

Hunate Engineers are the core life designers of the movement. We are seeking a multi-disciplinary team of young, skilled, and dedicated individuals to co-create and implement Hunateculture designs for our partner hubs. This is a deep commitment to walking the path and building the future.

Apply as an Engineer

Become a Hunate Mentor

Mentors are the guardians of wisdom in our movement. We are seeking respected, experienced professionals and elders from each engineering domain to serve as our advisory council. Your role is to guide our young team, provide ethical oversight, and lend your credibility to the mission.

Apply as a Mentor

Become a Hunate Volunteer

Volunteers are the lifeblood of our on-the-ground work, offering their time, energy, and skills as co-creators. You will provide hands-on support at our hubs—building, farming, teaching, and becoming part of our community family. A minimum 3-month commitment is required.

Apply as a Volunteer

Become a District Representative

District Representatives are the living bridge between the national movement and local communities. We need one male and one female representative from each district to be our eyes, ears, and heart on the ground, helping us build trust and identify partners.

Apply as a DR

Partner as a Hunate Hub

We are searching for 7 families and 7 orphanages across Nepal to become our first Hunateculture Hubs. If you have land, a deep desire for self-reliance, and the courage to become a living example of a regenerative future for your community, we invite you to apply.

Apply as a Hub
