Our Team

This movement is not built by one person, but by a dedicated family of visionaries, builders, and guardians who share a common vow.

The Founder

I am Khem Raj Pradhan, the visionary and founder of the HUNATE Movement. My role is to hold the integrity of the vision, to ask the hard questions, and to guide the team with the clarity born from my own journey. I am not the "boss"; I am the first student of this work, and my primary responsibility is to serve the mission and the team that makes it possible.

We Are Gathering the Hunate Engineers

The core of our work will be done by a team of young, multi-skilled "life designers." They will be the architects, permaculturists, astrologers, and healers who work directly with our partner hubs. I am not looking for employees with a specific degree; I am looking for co-creators who have a deep, lived understanding of their craft and an unwavering commitment to a better future.

This is a call for the brave. A call for those who seek purpose over profit, and mastery over mediocrity. In the future, this section will feature the profiles of the remarkable individuals who answer this call.

Seeking the Guardians of Wisdom (Mentors)

A movement without the wisdom of its elders is a tree without roots. I am actively seeking the guidance of respected professionals and community leaders to serve as Mentors for our young engineers. Your experience is the compass that will keep our work grounded in integrity, excellence, and long-term vision.
