The Catalog of Impact: Financial Contributions

Your financial contribution is a powerful seed. It allows us to procure exactly what is needed on the ground, support local economies, and empower our team to do their sacred work.

Nurturing Our Hubs (Families & Orphanages)

This is the heart of our mission. Your gift directly empowers our partners to build self-sufficient homes, farms, and lives. It is a direct investment in the grassroots resilience of Nepal.

  • The Gift of a Thriving Farm: Fund fruit trees, native seeds, irrigation systems, tools, ponds, and greenhouses.
  • The Gift of a Healthy Home: Contribute to safe, non-toxic kitchen setups and natural building materials.
  • The Gift of an Awakened Mind (Orphanages): Provide books, art supplies, musical instruments, and fund hands-on skill-building workshops.

Empowering Our Engineers (The Core Team)

Our Hunate Engineers are the dedicated heart and hands of this movement. Your support provides them with the tools and means to do their sacred work.

  • The Tools of a Life Designer: Fund essential technology like laptops, cameras, and data storage for design and storytelling.
  • The Journey of Service: Support the travel, food, and accommodation costs for our engineers during their crucial on-site visits.

Supporting Our Community (Mentors & Volunteers)

The movement is a web of relationships. Your gift helps us nurture the community that surrounds and supports our work, from the wise elders who guide us to the international volunteers who share their skills.

  • A Gift of Gratitude: Contribute to a fund for creating heartfelt gifts of appreciation for our Mentors.
  • A Safe & Nourishing Journey: Provide essential safety equipment and simple, healthy food for our on-site volunteers.

Building Our Future: The Hunate HQ Fund

Our ultimate vision is to build the Hunate HQ—a world-class, living campus for regenerative learning in Nepal. Your contribution here is a foundational investment in a legacy that will last for generations.

Become an In-Kind Sponsor

If you or your company can provide physical materials or professional services, you become a vital partner in this work. In-kind sponsorships are powerful, direct investments that reduce our costs and accelerate our progress on the ground.
