The Catalog of Impact

This is not a list of needs; it is a map of possibilities. Choose the future you wish to grow.

Nurturing Our Hubs (Families & Orphanages)

This is the heart of our mission. Your gift directly empowers our partners to build self-sufficient homes, farms, and lives. It is a direct investment in the grassroots resilience of Nepal.

  • The Gift of a Thriving Farm: Fund fruit trees, native seeds, irrigation systems, tools, ponds, and greenhouses.
  • The Gift of a Healthy Home: Contribute to safe, non-toxic kitchen setups and natural building materials (bamboo, mud, stone).
  • The Gift of an Awakened Mind (Orphanages): Provide books, art supplies, musical instruments, and fund hands-on skill-building workshops.

Empowering Our Engineers (The Core Team)

Our Hunate Engineers are the dedicated heart and hands of this movement. They are young, brilliant, and have committed their lives to this service. Your support for them is a direct investment in the success of the entire 7-7-7 Initiative.

  • The Tools of a Life Designer: Fund essential technology like laptops, cameras, and data storage for design and storytelling.
  • The Journey of Service: Support the travel, food, and accommodation costs for our engineers during their crucial on-site visits to remote hubs.
  • The Fuel for the Fire: Contribute to the modest stipends that allow our team to dedicate themselves fully to this mission.

Supporting Our Community (Mentors & Volunteers)

The movement is a web of relationships. Your gift helps us nurture the community that surrounds and supports our work, from the wise elders who guide us to the international volunteers who share their skills.

  • A Gift of Gratitude: Contribute to a fund for creating heartfelt gifts of appreciation for our Mentors.
  • A Safe & Nourishing Journey: Provide essential safety equipment and simple, healthy food and accommodation for our on-site volunteers.

Become an In-Kind Sponsor

If you or your company can provide physical materials or professional services, you become a vital partner in this work. In-kind sponsorships are powerful, direct investments that reduce our costs and accelerate our progress on the ground. Your brand will be honored as a foundational partner of the movement.

Farming & Seeds

Fruit trees, non-hybrid seeds, irrigation pipes, water pumps, organic fertilizers.

Building & Tools

Bamboo, sustainable timber, eco-friendly cement, carpentry tools, farming tools, safety equipment.

Energy & Tech

Solar panels, batteries, inverters, laptops, cameras, printers, and pro-bono web services.
