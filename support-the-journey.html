The Catalog of Impact

See exactly how your contribution can be transformed into tangible change.

For Our Hunate Hubs (Families & Orphanages)

Support the very heart of our mission. Your gift helps our partners build self-sufficient homes and farms.

  • The Gift of a Thriving Farm: Fund fruit trees, seeds, irrigation systems, tools, ponds, and greenhouses.
  • The Gift of a Healthy Home: Contribute to safe, non-toxic kitchen setups and natural building materials.
  • The Gift of an Awakened Mind (Orphanages): Provide books, art supplies, musical instruments, and fund hands-on workshops.

For Our Hunate Engineers (The Core Team)

Empower the young life designers who are the engine of this movement. Your support provides them with the tools and means to do their sacred work.

  • The Tools of a Life Designer: Fund essential technology like laptops, cameras, and data storage.
  • The Journey of Service: Support the travel, food, and accommodation costs for our engineers during their crucial on-site visits.
  • The Fuel for the Fire: Contribute to the modest stipends that allow our team to dedicate themselves fully to this mission.

For Our Volunteers & Mentors (The Community)

Nurture the community that surrounds and supports our work. Your gift helps us care for those who give their time and wisdom so generously.

  • A Gift of Gratitude: Contribute to a fund for creating heartfelt gifts of appreciation for our Mentors.
  • A Safe Journey: Provide essential safety equipment and simple, healthy food and accommodation for our on-site volunteers.
