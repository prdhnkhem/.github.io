Phase 5: Building The 7 Provinces

This is where the blueprint meets the ground. Our first tangible mission is to move from theory to reality by establishing a decentralized network of living, breathing examples of Hunateculture across the nation of Nepal.

The "7-7-7 Initiative": A Nationwide Proof of Concept

The goal of Phase 5 is simple, yet profound. We call it the "7-7-7 Initiative." Our mission is to identify and form a sacred partnership with **one family (a Hunate Hub Partner)** and **one local orphanage** in each of Nepal's **seven provinces**. This is not about building seven identical farms; it is about planting seven unique seeds of regeneration.

Each family hub will become a living model of Hunateculture, adapted to its unique local climate and culture. They will be lighthouses, demonstrating to their entire region that a life of health, self-reliance, and harmony with nature is not a forgotten dream, but a practical and achievable reality. By partnering with a local orphanage at each location, we infuse the project with deep purpose and inter-generational connection. The hubs become not just places of production, but centers of learning, service, and love, providing stability, skills, and a sense of belonging for children in need.

This decentralized network is our proof of concept. It is how we will show that the Hunate blueprint is not a rigid dogma, but an adaptable science that can empower communities everywhere.

The Search for Our Co-Creators

This mission will not be imposed from the top-down; it will grow from the grassroots up. We must be clear: this is a sacred partnership, not a charity project. We do not offer handouts. The Hunate Movement provides the most valuable things we have: a complete design blueprint for a regenerative life (Hunateculture), expert training from our dedicated Engineers and Mentors, and access to a global support network. In return, our Hub Partners provide the most essential elements: the land, the heart, the unwavering commitment, and the courage to walk this path with us. Together, we become co-creators of a new reality.
