Support the Work

This is a shared mission. Your support is a direct investment in the tools, seeds, and people who are building a new world from the ground up.

A Simple, Transparent Process

1

Choose Your Path

Decide whether you'd like to make a financial donation or provide an in-kind sponsorship of materials. Explore our funding priorities below to see where your support can make the most impact.

2

Make Your Contribution

For financial support, use the direct bank transfer details provided. For in-kind sponsorships, prepare the details of the materials you can offer. This is the act of planting the seed.

3

Register Your Support (Crucial)

This is the most important step. Fill out our official Donor & Sponsor Form. This allows me to track every contribution, log it correctly in our public ledger, and personally thank you for your trust.

Our Funding & Sponsorship Priorities

Your contribution is never just a number; it is a tangible piece of the future we are building. Here is exactly what your support helps create.

Foundational Support for Hunate Hubs

This is the core of our on-the-ground work, directly empowering families and orphanages.

  • Seeds & Saplings: For planting a family's first food forest.
  • Farming Tools: The basic equipment for regenerative agriculture.
  • Building Materials: Natural materials like bamboo, stone, and wood for eco-homes.
  • A Single Goat or Cow: Providing a family with a source of nourishment and fertility.
  • A Small Solar Setup: Giving a home clean, reliable light for children to study by.

Empowering the Hunate Engineers

This supports the core team who designs and guides the transformation at each hub.

  • Travel Fund: Covering the cost of transport to remote hub locations.
  • Digital Tools: Subscriptions for design software and collaborative platforms.
  • Training Materials: Books and resources for their continued learning.
  • Field Equipment: Soil testing kits, cameras for documentation, and other essential gear.

The Future Fund (Hunate HQ)

For long-term visionaries who want to invest in the permanent home of this movement.

  • Land Acquisition Fund: The seed money to secure a permanent piece of land.
  • Architectural Design: Supporting the creation of the final blueprints.
  • Core Infrastructure: Funding the first water systems, energy grids, and foundational buildings.
