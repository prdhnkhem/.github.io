Support the Work

This movement is fueled by community trust, not corporate funding. Every contribution is a direct investment in the soil, the people, and the future of Nepal.

A Note on Our Philosophy: We are an unregistered, citizen-led movement. We build trust through radical transparency, not government paperwork. Every rupee is tracked publicly, and every resource is honored. When you support us, you are not a donor; you are a co-creator.

Ways You Can Contribute

Support can take many forms. Below are the most direct ways to fuel this work. We believe in channeling resources with clear intention, so you can choose exactly which part of the movement you wish to nourish.

1. Financial Contributions

Your financial support allows us the flexibility to direct resources where they are needed most. This is the lifeblood for our team's travel, training, and the materials needed on the ground.

Support a Hunate Hub

Directly fund the transformation of a family or orphanage. Your contribution can provide seeds, building tools, or help establish their ethical business.

Support an Engineer

Enable our core team members to travel, conduct research, and dedicate their time to the hubs by covering their basic costs for food and transport.

Support the HQ Vision

Contribute to the future foundation—the land, tools, and materials needed to build the central HUNATE Headquarters, our living classroom for the world.

2. Sponsor Specific Materials (In-Kind)

Resources are as vital as funds. If you or your business can provide any of the following high-quality materials, you can become a direct builder of our hubs.

  • Farming Tools: Quality spades, pipes, water pumps, greenhouse materials.
  • Construction Tools: Drills, saws, safety equipment, and raw materials like wood, bamboo, or stone.
  • Plants & Seeds: Native, non-GMO seeds for fruits, nuts, herbs, and vegetables.
  • Kitchen & Home: Non-toxic cookware (iron, clay, brass), and other household essentials.
  • Digital Tools: Laptops, cameras, or hard drives for our engineers and media team.
  • Energy Systems: Solar panels, batteries, biogas system components.
