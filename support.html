My Vow of Radical Transparency

I have chosen to operate this movement as a citizen, not as a registered NGO. My accountability is not to a government office; it is to you—my community. I build trust not with paperwork, but with undeniable proof. Every rupee received and every rupee spent on this work is documented here, live and in public, for the world to see.

This is my personal bank account, used with full transparency for this mission. Every transaction is logged and made public within 24 hours.
