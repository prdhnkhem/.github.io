In-Kind Support (Sponsor)

Resources and tools are as vital as money. If you or your organization can provide materials, you are directly helping us build the physical foundation of a new Hunate Hub.

Our Current Needs for the Work:

  • For the Soil: Native fruit tree saplings, quality shovels, rakes, and irrigation pipes.
  • For Building: Natural materials like bamboo, stone, and wood; essential construction tools.
  • For Learning: Laptops and cameras for my engineers; books and supplies for children in our partner orphanages.
  • For Energy: Solar panels, batteries, and components for biogas systems.

If you can help with any of these items, please begin the conversation by filling out our official form.
