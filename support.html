Support the Work

This is not a charity. It is a shared mission. Your support is a direct investment in the tools, seeds, and people who are building a new world from the ground up.

A Simple, Transparent Process

To maintain integrity and clarity, I have designed a simple three-step process for anyone who wishes to contribute to this work.

  1. Choose Your Path: Decide whether you'd like to make a financial donation or provide an in-kind sponsorship of materials. Explore our funding priorities below to see the direct impact.
  2. Make Your Contribution: For financial support, use the direct bank transfer details. For in-kind sponsorships, note the materials you can offer. This is the act of planting the seed.
  3. Register Your Support (Crucial): This is the most important step. Fill out our official Donor Form. This allows me to track every contribution, log it correctly in our public ledger, and personally thank you for your trust.

Funding & Sponsorship Priorities

Your contribution is never just a number; it is a tangible piece of the future we are building. Here is exactly what your support helps create.

I. Foundational Support for Hunate Hubs

This is the core of our on-the-ground work, directly empowering families and orphanages.

  • Seeds & Saplings: For planting a family's first food forest.
  • Farming Tools: The basic equipment for regenerative agriculture.
  • Building Materials: Natural materials like bamboo, stone, and wood for eco-homes.
  • A Single Goat or Cow: Providing a family with a source of nourishment and fertility.
  • A Small Solar Setup: Giving a home clean, reliable light for children to study by.

II. Empowering the Hunate Engineers

This supports the core team who designs and guides the transformation at each hub.

  • Travel Fund: Covering the cost of transport to remote hub locations.
  • Digital Tools: Subscriptions for design software and collaborative platforms.
  • Field Equipment: Soil testing kits, cameras for documentation, and other essential gear.
