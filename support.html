Support the Work

This is not a charity. This is a shared mission. Your support is a direct investment in the tools, seeds, and people who are building a new world from the ground up.

My Vow of Radical Transparency

I have chosen to operate this movement as a citizen, not as a registered NGO. I believe the systems of regulation and bureaucracy often create more problems than they solve. My accountability is not to a government office; it is to you. I build trust not with paperwork, but with undeniable proof.

Every contribution I receive and every rupee I spend on this work is documented in a public ledger, updated every 24 hours. My finances, like my philosophy, are an open book.

In-Kind Support (Sponsor)
